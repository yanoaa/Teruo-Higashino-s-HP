Information about How to Reach Higashino Laboratory in SUITA Campus


Access to Higashino Lab. in SUITA Campus from Shin-Osaka Station and Itami/Kansai Airports (Detailed information is shown by clicking this map)


Higashino Lab. is located at 5th floor of Building “A” of Graduate School of Information Science and Technology in SUITA Campus. The building is located at about 100 south from the bus stop of "Handai Honbu-mae”. It is located at the west of Osaka University's Convention Center. A wider map is shown by clicking this map.
